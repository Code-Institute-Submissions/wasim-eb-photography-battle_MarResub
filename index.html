Photographers battle

If you are a photographer and you are looking for a community and a fun way to get inspiration, this is your kind of event

About the event

In this event we will gather six photographers to participate in the first ever photographers battle in Stockholm. There are three different stages of the event. The first one takes off in Kungsholmen where the each photographer will get 60 minutes to get shoot the perfect picture. After this, each photographer will pick their best photo that they would like to submit and the followers of our instagram-page will decide four winners to go on to the next challenge. Same principle applies to the second challenge which takes place at the Central Station. The last challenge will only have two remaining contenders, and it takes place at Sodermalm, the south side of stockholm. Winner is announced on our instagram-page.
